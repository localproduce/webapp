About LocalSzn

LocalSzn uses publicly available data covering weekly wholesale market prices across Canada collected by Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada. This dataset is updated weekly, and entries are updated in our local database through the open.canada.ca API. Through user-provided location data and real-time produce data, Localszn prioritizes local choices from nearly 1,000 different produce variants of ranging prices to ensure a wide scope of selection for users. Localszn aims to encourage users to make local, informed, and sustainable choices when it comes to buying day-to-day groceries, supporting local businesses in the process.
